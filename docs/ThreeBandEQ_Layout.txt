## Module Dimensions
- **Width**: 18HP (270mm / 10.63 inches)
- **Height**: 128.5mm (standard Eurorack 3U)

## Display Positions
- **Frequency Response**: X=85, Y=20, Size=200×80
- **Gain Meters**: X=85, Y=110, Size=200×60

## Control Layout
- **Left Column**: 5 CV inputs (vertical alignment)
- **Center Column**: 5 parameter knobs + bypass switch
- **Right Side**: Two visualization displays (stacked)
- **Bottom**: Audio I/O (2 inputs, 2 outputs)
